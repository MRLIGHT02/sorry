I'm Sorry

for being cold when you needed warmth

I know my replies have been short —
sharp around the edges when they shouldn't have been.

Being busy isn't an excuse for being cold.
I should have been more patient.
I should have been softer.

When you go quiet, I worry.
I just want to know what's going on in your world —
even if it's just a little line, a tiny window.
But I promise to be gentler in asking.

You deserve my patience, not my haste.
You deserve my warmth, not my chill.

❀ ✿ ❀

Always here for you,
ready to listen whenever you're ready to share.

Yours
💙
